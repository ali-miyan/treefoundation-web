Tree Foundation india

Tree Foundation India, established by a social activist, Haseebe Ahammed, is operational since 2018. His journeys to rural areas of different states in India decided the proximity of work for marginalized sectors on education, health, and other development sectors. This grassroots activity addressed the immediate needs of rural Indian villages. Later, a group of passionate and committed individuals joined and formed a helping hand to bring about significant changes in these vulnerable communities. The essence of Tree Foundation India lies in its focus on the United Nation's Sustainable Development Goals, particularly in areas such as women empowerment, education, environment, health, and responsible tourism.
