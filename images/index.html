Tree Foundation india

Tree Foundation India, established in 2018 by Haseebe Ahammed, embarked on a journey to specific rural areas in India. This grassroots journey revealed to him the genuine needs of rural Indian villages. Later a group of passionate and committed individuals joined and formed to bring about significant change in these communities. The essence of the Tree Foundation India lies in its focus on the UN's Sustainable Development Goals, particularly in areas such as women empowerment, education, environment, health, and sustainable tourism.
